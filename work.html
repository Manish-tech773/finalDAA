Problem Statement

Smart City Solutions for Efficient Urban Management in Chandigarh.

We are planning to develop a smart city named Chandigarh, utilizing the knowledge gained through our Data Analytics and Architecture (DAA) course. The city will be built on a 1500-square-meter area and will integrate advanced technologies to enhance urban living.

The design will feature at least six business models aimed at fostering sustainable economic growth, innovation, and social well-being.

By leveraging cutting-edge technologies, we strive to create a sustainable and citizen-centric urban environment.
